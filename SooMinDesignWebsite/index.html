ABOUT ME

Born in Seoul, South Korea, and raised in Toronto, Sacha thrives and is inspired by the energy and vibrancy of both cultures. Growing up in such diverse environments, Sacha developed a keen appreciation for multiculturalism and a deep understanding of the complexities of modern urban living. This fusion of influences is evident in Sacha's creative endeavors, from art and fashion to music and cuisine. With an insatiable curiosity and a passion for exploration, Sacha constantly seeks out new experiences, drawing inspiration from the vibrant streets of Seoul and the eclectic neighborhoods of Toronto.
